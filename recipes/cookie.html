Chocolate Chip Cookie





~Description~

Chocolate chip cookies are classic, chewy treats made by creaming together butter and sugar, then mixing in eggs, vanilla, flour, and baking soda. Semi-sweet chocolate chips are folded into the dough before scooping and baking until golden brown. The result is a soft, slightly crisp cookie with melty chocolate pockets and a rich, buttery flavor.


~Ingredients~

    • All-purpose flour - 2 ¼ cups
    • Baking soda - 1 teaspoon
    • Salt - ½ teaspoon
    • Unsalted butter - 1 cup (softened)
    • Granulated sugar - ¾ cup
    • Brown sugar (light or dark) - ¾ cup (packed)
    • Eggs - 2 large
    • Vanilla extract - 1 teaspoon
    • Semi-sweet chocolate chips - 2 cups (or about 340g)

    ~Steps~

    1. Preheat the oven:

      Preheat your oven to 175°C (350°F). Line a baking tray with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat.

    2. Mix dry ingredients:

      In a medium bowl, whisk together:

      • Flour
      • Baking Soda
      • Salt

      Set it aside.

    3. Cream butter and sugars:

      In a large bowl, use a hand mixer or stand mixer to beat together butter, granulated sugar and brown sugar.
      Beat on medium speed for 2-3 minutes until light and creamy.

    4. Add eggs and vanilla:

      Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each. Add the vanilla extract and beat until fully combined.

    5. Add dry ingredients:

      Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, mixing on low speed or by hand until just combined. Don’t overmix.

    6. Fold in chocolate chips:

      Using a spatula, fold in the chocolate chips until evenly distributed.

    7. Shape the cookies:

      Scoop dough using a tablespoon or cookie scoop. Roll into balls and place them 2 inches apart on the prepared baking tray.

    8. Bake!

      Bake in the preheated oven for 10–12 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden but the centers still look slightly soft.

    9. Let it cool down:

      Let the cookies cool on the tray for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.


    ~Now you have warm, gooey, delicious chocolate chip cookies—perfect for sharing or enjoying all by yourself! 🍪
