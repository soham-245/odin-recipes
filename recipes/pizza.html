Margherita Pizza





~Description~

Margherita pizza is a simple yet flavorful Italian classic made with a thin, crispy crust topped with fresh tomato sauce, slices of mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil leaves. It's baked until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the crust is golden.
